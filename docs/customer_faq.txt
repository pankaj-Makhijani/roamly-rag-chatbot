PACKAGE AND PRICING QUESTIONS

Q: Are the prices on your website per person or for the entire package?
A: All prices listed are per person on twin sharing basis (two people sharing one room). Solo traveler pricing is higher due to single room supplement. We will always confirm the exact price based on your group size before booking.

Q: What does twin sharing mean?
A: Twin sharing means two people share one hotel room. If you are traveling alone and want a private room, a single room supplement charge applies, which varies by destination and hotel.

Q: Can I get a customized quote for a group?
A: Yes. Groups of 10 or more travelers get special pricing with up to 12% discount. Contact us on WhatsApp at +91-98765-43210 for a group quote.

Q: Are flights included in the package price?
A: Most of our international packages include return flights from Ahmedabad or Mumbai. The specific departure city is mentioned in each package. Domestic packages include either train or flight depending on the package. Always check the inclusions section of each package for clarity.

Q: Can I upgrade my hotel to a 5-star?
A: Yes, hotel upgrades are available for most packages at an additional cost. Contact us before booking to get the upgrade pricing for your chosen destination.

Q: Do you offer honeymoon packages?
A: Yes. We offer honeymoon packages with special inclusions like room upgrades, flower decoration, welcome amenities, and couple spa sessions. Popular honeymoon destinations include Bali, Maldives, Kerala, and Europe. Contact us to customize your dream honeymoon.

VISA QUESTIONS

Q: Do you help with visa applications?
A: Yes. Roamly provides complete visa assistance for UAE, Schengen (Europe), Thailand, and Singapore visas. Our visa team has a 98% approval rate. Visa fees are charged separately and are non-refundable once submitted.

Q: How long does visa processing take?
A: UAE visa takes 3-4 working days. Thailand e-Visa takes 3-5 working days. Schengen visa takes 15-20 working days. Always apply well in advance. For Europe trips, we recommend starting the visa process at least 6 weeks before travel.

Q: Which countries can Indian passport holders visit without a visa?
A: Bali (Indonesia) and Maldives offer visa on arrival to Indian passport holders. Thailand also has visa on arrival but Roamly recommends applying for an e-Visa in advance to avoid airport queues.

Q: My passport expires in 4 months. Can I still travel?
A: Most countries require your passport to be valid for at least 6 months beyond your return date. A passport expiring in 4 months may not be accepted. We recommend renewing your passport before booking any international travel.

PAYMENT QUESTIONS

Q: How much deposit is required to confirm a booking?
A: A non-refundable booking deposit of 25% of the total package cost is required to confirm your booking. The remaining 75% must be paid at least 30 days before departure for international packages.

Q: Do you offer EMI options?
A: Yes. No-cost EMI is available on HDFC and ICICI credit cards for bookings above ₹50,000, with 6 and 12 month EMI options.

Q: Is UPI payment accepted?
A: Yes. You can pay via UPI at roamly@hdfcbank. We also accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cash at our Vadodara office.

CANCELLATION AND REFUND QUESTIONS

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: For international packages, cancellation 45 or more days before departure gives a full refund minus the 25% deposit. Cancellation between 30-44 days gets a 50% refund. Cancellation between 15-29 days gets a 25% refund. Cancellation less than 15 days before departure has no refund. Domestic packages have similar but slightly shorter notice periods.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 working days to your original payment method.

Q: Can I change my travel dates after booking?
A: Yes, date changes are allowed subject to availability. A date change fee of ₹2,000 per person applies for international packages if requested 30 or more days before departure. Changes within 30 days attract a higher fee of ₹3,500 per person plus any fare difference.

ON-TRIP SUPPORT QUESTIONS

Q: Will someone from Roamly be available if I face issues during the trip?
A: Yes. Roamly provides a 24/7 emergency helpline number in your travel kit. A dedicated trip coordinator is assigned to every booking and can be reached on WhatsApp at any time during your trip.

Q: What if my flight gets delayed or cancelled?
A: Your trip coordinator will help you rebook or rearrange the itinerary. Travel insurance is strongly recommended as it covers trip disruptions, additional accommodation, and rebooking costs.

Q: What is included in the travel kit Roamly sends before departure?
A: Your travel kit includes the final itinerary, hotel vouchers, flight e-tickets, visa documents, local emergency contact numbers, currency exchange tips, packing list, and destination-specific travel tips.
