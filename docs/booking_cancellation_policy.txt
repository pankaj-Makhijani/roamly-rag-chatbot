ROAMLY - BOOKING AND CANCELLATION POLICY

---

BOOKING PROCESS

Step 1: Package Selection
Browse our packages on roamly.in or visit our office in Vadodara. You can also WhatsApp us at +91-98765-43210 to discuss your requirements. Our travel experts will suggest the best package based on your budget, travel dates, and preferences.

Step 2: Booking Confirmation
To confirm your booking, a non-refundable booking deposit of 25% of the total package cost is required. This deposit secures your flights, hotels, and visa processing slot.

Step 3: Document Submission
Submit all required documents (passport copies, photographs, bank statements) within 48 hours of paying the deposit. Delay in document submission may affect visa processing timelines.

Step 4: Final Payment
The remaining 75% of the package cost must be paid at least 30 days before the departure date for international packages and 15 days before for domestic packages.

Step 5: Travel Kit
Roamly sends a complete travel kit 5 days before departure including final itinerary, hotel vouchers, flight tickets, visa documents, local contact numbers, and emergency helpline details.

- Bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS/RTGS) to Roamly's official account
- UPI payment (roamly@hdfcbank)

- Cash payment at our Vadodara office (Alkapuri, Vadodara - 390007)

---

CANCELLATION POLICY

International Packages:
- Cancellation 45 or more days before departure: Full refund minus booking deposit (25%)
- Cancellation 30-44 days before departure: 50% refund of total package cost
- Cancellation 15-29 days before departure: 25% refund of total package cost
- Cancellation less than 15 days before departure: No refund
- No-show on departure date: No refund

Domestic Packages:
- Cancellation 30 or more days before departure: Full refund minus booking deposit (25%)
- Cancellation 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund of total package cost
- Cancellation 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund of total package cost
- Cancellation less than 7 days before departure: No refund

Note: Visa fees, travel insurance premiums, and any third-party booking fees are non-refundable regardless of cancellation timing, as these are paid directly to embassies or insurance providers.

---

DATE CHANGE POLICY
- Date changes requested 30 or more days before departure: Allowed subject to availability, with a date change fee of ₹2,000 per person for international packages and ₹1,000 per person for domestic packages. Fare difference (if any) will be charged.
- Date changes requested less than 30 days before departure: Subject to airline and hotel availability. Fare difference plus ₹3,500 per person amendment fee applies.
- Date changes are not guaranteed and depend on flight and hotel availability at the time of request.

---

REFUND PROCESS
Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 working days to the original payment method. For bank transfers, refunds are processed to the account from which payment was received. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 working days to reflect depending on the card issuer.

---

GROUP BOOKING POLICY
For groups of 10 or more travelers, a dedicated group coordinator is assigned. Group bookings require a 30% deposit at the time of booking. Cancellation of individual members from a group booking will be treated as individual cancellations per the policy above. If the group size drops below 10 after confirmation, group discount pricing will be revised accordingly.

---

TRAVEL INSURANCE
Roamly strongly recommends purchasing travel insurance for all international trips. Travel insurance covers trip cancellation, medical emergencies abroad, loss of baggage, flight delays, and passport loss. Insurance is mandatory for Schengen visa applications. Roamly offers travel insurance starting at ₹999 per person for domestic trips and ₹1,800 per person for international trips up to 12 days. Insurance premiums are non-refundable once the policy is issued.

---

FORCE MAJEURE
In cases of natural disasters, political unrest, pandemic-related travel restrictions, or other events beyond Roamly's control, we will work with customers to reschedule travel at no additional cost. Refunds in force majeure situations depend on recovery of costs from airlines, hotels, and other vendors, which may take 30-90 days.
