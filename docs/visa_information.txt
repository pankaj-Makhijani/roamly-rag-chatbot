ROAMLY - VISA INFORMATION GUIDE FOR INDIAN PASSPORT HOLDERS

This guide covers visa requirements for all destinations featured in Roamly packages. Information is accurate as of 2025. Always verify with the respective embassy before travel as visa rules can change.

---

VISA ON ARRIVAL (No advance visa required)

BALI, INDONESIA
- Visa type: Visa on Arrival (VOA)
- Fee: USD 35 (approximately ₹2,900), payable at Bali Ngurah Rai Airport
- Validity: 30 days, extendable once for another 30 days
- Requirements: Return ticket, hotel booking confirmation, passport valid for 6 months
- Processing time: 15-30 minutes at the airport counter
- Roamly tip: Carry USD cash for the visa counter. Card payments are accepted but USD cash is faster.

MALDIVES
- Visa type: Free visa on arrival
- Fee: No charge
- Validity: 30 days
- Requirements: Return ticket, hotel/resort booking, proof of sufficient funds
- Processing time: Instant on arrival
- Roamly tip: No action needed before travel. Just show your resort booking at immigration.

THAILAND (E-Visa recommended but VOA available)
- Visa type: Visa on Arrival available at major airports
- Fee: THB 2,000 (approximately ₹4,800) for VOA
- Validity: 15 days (VOA), 60 days (Tourist e-Visa)
- Roamly strongly recommends applying for Thailand e-Visa in advance to avoid long queues at Bangkok Suvarnabhumi Airport, especially during peak season.
- E-Visa fee: ₹4,500 (processed by Roamly within 3-5 working days)

VISA REQUIRED IN ADVANCE

DUBAI, UAE
- Visa type: UAE Tourist Visa (mandatory for Indian passport holders)
- Fee: ₹6,500 per person (30-day single entry), processed by Roamly
- Validity: 30 days from date of entry
- Processing time: 3-4 working days after submission of documents
- Documents required:
  * Passport copy (valid for minimum 6 months)
  * Passport-size photograph (white background)
  * Confirmed return flight tickets
  * Hotel booking confirmation
  * Last 3 months bank statement showing minimum balance of ₹50,000
- Roamly handles the entire UAE visa process. You just need to submit documents 10 days before travel.
- Roamly tip: UAE visa is linked to your airline. Roamly coordinates visa with your flight booking automatically.

EUROPE (SCHENGEN VISA)
- Visa type: Schengen Tourist Visa
- Countries covered: France, Switzerland, Netherlands, Germany, Italy, Spain, and 22 other European countries
- Fee: Approximately ₹9,500 per person (embassy fee, non-refundable)
- Validity: Usually issued for duration of trip, sometimes with extra days
- Processing time: 15-20 working days (apply minimum 6 weeks before travel, maximum 6 months before)
- Documents required:
  * Original passport (valid for 3 months beyond return date)
  * Passport-size photographs (35mm x 45mm, white background, recent)
  * Confirmed return flight tickets
  * Hotel bookings for entire stay
  * Travel insurance covering minimum EUR 30,000 (mandatory)
  * Last 6 months bank statements
  * Income proof (salary slips, ITR, Form 16)
  * Cover letter explaining purpose of travel
  * NOC from employer (if salaried) or business proof (if self-employed)
- Roamly provides complete Schengen visa documentation support and checklist. We do not guarantee visa approval as it is at the sole discretion of the embassy.
- Roamly tip: Apply for the country where you spend the most nights. For our Paris + Switzerland + Amsterdam package, apply at the French consulate.
- Travel insurance: Roamly offers Schengen-compliant travel insurance at ₹1,800 per person for 10-12 day trips.

SINGAPORE
- Visa type: Singapore Tourist Visa
- Fee: SGD 30 (approximately ₹1,900), non-refundable
- Processing time: 3-5 working days
- Documents required: Passport, photograph, bank statement, return ticket, hotel booking
- Roamly tip: Singapore visa can be applied online via ICA website. Roamly assists with documentation.

SRI LANKA
- Visa type: Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA)
- Fee: USD 20 (approximately ₹1,700)
- Processing time: Usually instant, sometimes up to 24 hours
- Apply online at: eta.gov.lk
- Roamly tip: Very straightforward process. Roamly can assist if needed.

DOMESTIC TRAVEL
No visa required for any domestic destination. Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport) required at airports and hotels.

---

PASSPORT VALIDITY RULE
Most countries require your passport to be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel return date. Always check your passport expiry before booking. Roamly will flag passport validity issues during the booking process.
